FAQ

I became interested in taking photos after discovering my familys old photo album. It made me a little emotional looking at photos when I was younger, so I thought it would be nice to start making a photo album for myself to look back on in the future.

Personally, I don't take on photography projects and like to take photos casually. I will generally only bring my small cameras if I plan to leave the city for a trip. I do want to take more photos this year and hopefully fill up the photo album before 2025.
